About me

I was born and raised in a beautiful seaside city, where my passion for water sports began at my family’s sea sports club. Growing up surrounded by the ocean, I naturally gravitated towards windsurfing, sailing, and kayaking. Today, I am proud to be a windsurfing coach, sharing my love for the sport with others. Over the years, I’ve competed and won numerous windsurfing prizes, both in Morocco and internationally.

Alongside my passion for sea sports, I am pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Information Systems Engineering at Vilnius Tech. This blend of technology and my maritime heritage inspires me to keep exploring new challenges and sharing my story with others.
